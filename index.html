Marcelo Fischborn

Marcelo Fischborn is an early career teacher and researcher of philosophy at the Farroupilha Federal Institute of Education, Science, and Technology, in Brazil. His research focuses mainly on responsibility practices, free will, and moral psychology.

You can reach me at marcelofischborn@gmail.com.

  • How should free will skeptics pursue legal change? Neuroethics (2018).
  • Libet-style experiments, neuroscience, and libertarian free will, Philosophical Psychology (2016).
  • Neuroscience and the possibility of locally determined choices: Reply to Adina Roskies and Eddy Nahmias. Philosophical Psychology (2017).
